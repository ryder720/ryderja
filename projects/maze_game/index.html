    How to Play

    Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move your character (Blue Square) through the maze.

    Collect Letters (Green Text) scattered in the maze.

    Approach Doors (Red Squares) and a dialog will appear. Enter the correct word using your collected letters to unlock it. Opened doors turn Gold.

    The list of Possible Words on the right will initially be hidden by asterisks. Collected letters will reveal themselves in this list.

    The goal is to unlock all doors to complete the level!

    Your view is limited! Walls will block your line of sight.
